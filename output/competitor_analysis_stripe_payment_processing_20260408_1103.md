- Business banking: checking, savings, loans
- Staff management and payroll integration

**Unique Capabilities (not in most competitors):**
- Industry-specific POS modes (restaurants with table and course management, retail with advanced inventory, service bookings)
- Comprehensive hardware ecosystem tailored for different business needs
- Seamless integration of POS with email/text marketing, banking, and payroll within the same ecosystem
- Offline payments support for uninterrupted sales

**Key Integrations:**
- Cannot locate a dedicated public integrations page; Square is known for strong native integrations within its ecosystem (e.g., Payroll, Marketing, Online Store)
- Developer SDKs/APIs allow custom integrations and extensions (payments, POS, orders, customers)

**What Users Love:**  
- Ease of use and quick setup: "Square simplifies payment processing and invoicing, and is very easy to get started with."  
- Versatile POS suitable for many industries and seamless hardware support  
- Robust reporting and business insights at the point of sale  
- Good developer tools and API for custom solutions

**What Users Hate:**  
- Occasionally account holds and flags for higher risk businesses reported in online discussions  
- Fees can be higher than some traditional processors for certain use cases  
- Some users report occasional stability issues or limitations with offline mode duration

**Developer Experience:**  
- Comprehensive API documentation available at https://developer.squareup.com/docs  
- Well-documented Payments API, Orders API, POS API with example requests  
- Multiple SDKs available for easy integration with popular languages

- Good developer community and tooling to build custom payment applications and integrate Square services

**Feature Gap vs Stripe:**  
- What Square has that Stripe lacks:  
  - Industry-tailored POS systems with hardware options  
  - Built-in marketing, payroll, business banking, and inventory management within POS ecosystem

- What Stripe has that Square lacks:  
  - Global scale with support for a broader array of international payment methods and currencies  
  - Advanced, developer-centric APIs focused on complex payment flows and financial products
